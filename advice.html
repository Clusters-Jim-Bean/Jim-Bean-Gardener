February Advice

With spring drawing ever closer its time to get started with your jobs for February:

Take hardwood cuttings

Take hardwood cuttings from holly, dogwoods and willows for a cheap and easy way to create new plants for your garden

Prepare the greenhouse

Spring clean the greenhouse to give your new seedlings the best start. Clean windows, tidy up and remove any hiding slugs

Mulch beds and borders

Mulch beds and borders to supress weeds, warm soil and preserve moisture to give your plants the best start

Prune Trees

Now's a great time of year to prune trees and shrubs while they're dormant. Try to create an open shape and remove any damaged or diseased braches.

Protect tender plants

With the chance of late frosts possible, it's important to protect tender plants like passifloras, tree ferns and pieris japonicas.

Start Sowing Sweet Peas

Start Sowing Sweet Peas for an early flowering display, keep sowing at regular intervals to prolong your flowering until Autumn
